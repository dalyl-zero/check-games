How to Play

CheckGames Gameplay

Learn how to play CheckGames.

Each player starts with 5 cards

The primary objective of the game is to outpace your opponents by being the first to discard all the cards in your hand. Each player starts with a set number of cards, and through strategic play and careful consideration of each move, they must work towards emptying their hand.

Players take turns drawing and playing cards

Gameplay revolves around the discard pile, which dictates the pace and direction of the game. To make a move, you must play a card from your hand that corresponds in rank or suit to the top card on the discard pile. For example, if the top card is a five of hearts, you can play any five or any heart from your hand. If none of your cards corresponds, you have to pick a card from the deck and pass your turn.

The game ends when a player's hand reach zero.

Victory is achieved when a player successfully discards their last card, leaving them with an empty hand. As the final card is played, the game immediately concludes, and the player who managed to rid themselves of all cards first is declared the winner. Congratulations!

The player with no remaining cards is the winner.

Congratulations! You've mastered the game. Time to play again!
